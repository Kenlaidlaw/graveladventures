Welcome to our Gravel Adventures

Ken and Elaines adventures - gravel biking and trail running

Welcome to our blog site -

This site tracks our routes with some pictures and maps and hopefully some photos of dubious quality. We would have started this a year ago when we got our shiny new bicycles but covid got in the way. Hopefully the coming year will be better and we can bring you some news and reviews of amazing and wild places.
